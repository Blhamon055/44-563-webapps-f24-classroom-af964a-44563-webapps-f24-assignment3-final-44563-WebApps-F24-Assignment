My Superhero name...

Blue Longwomen the Wise...

Blue Longwoman the Wise was trained at birth to learn philosophy in the most excruciating way. Her own father was killed by the own principle of Brudian's Ass. For her father was a goat after quarrelling with the fearsome Doctor Strange.

Some powers of Blue Longwoman the Wise are:

  • Able to philosophically disarm her enemies
  • Can stretch up to 30 feet in length
  • Can manipulate the eyes of her enemies into only seeing the color blue, eventually driving them mad
